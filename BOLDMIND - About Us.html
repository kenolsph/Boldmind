About Us

Boldmind Integrated Resources is a young and dynamic media company that specializes in indoor and Out of Home advertising. We are ultimately inspired by our client’s message and demand a competitive edge in the marketplace by clearly delivering their vision to their audience because we believe that elevated positioning, economic success, and market presence for companies is developed from strategic thinking, a smart approach, creative insight and a passion for positioning. Consequently, our intuition and experience are combined to create an authentic brand story.



OUR MAIN FOCUS

Outdoor Advertising

Our mission is to elevate the entire out-of-home medium, by connecting advertisers with the best assets, audiences, innovations, and people in the business. Our commitment is to always be out front and ahead of the curve, thereby helping our clients grow and prosper

Branding

We create expressions for brands that are unique, unambiguous and consistent, and initiate participation through a series of activities that are designed to inform,engross, transform and motivate businesses. We nurturebrand growth through design development, identity creation and rejuvenation.

OOH Advertisement

We are a full-service Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising company that believes brands should achieve their campaign and maximize their returns from Out- of-Home (OOH) campaigns on minimal budget. We professionally plan and execute client’s campaign, growing their businesses via strategic brand exposures.
